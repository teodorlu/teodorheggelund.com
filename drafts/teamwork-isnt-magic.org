Why should it be? It is not.

Design and iteration. Trust. I don't trust that much. But perhaps is that due to
the team composition? I have placed myself in places where I am able to make the
bulk of the decisions from an early age. Often because I have been the best
candidate for the job.

Should I make that change? Perhaps, yes. I have things to learn.
