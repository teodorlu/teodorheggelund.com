author: Teodor Heggelund

You're probably wondering what biblical stories have to do with technology
development. I'm going to start there.

The scope of an application expands until it is unusable. The mud grows in all
directions. More material is added. More mud is built to ensure a proper
foundation of the previous mud. And since the mud gradually collapses, even more
in-between mud shows up.
